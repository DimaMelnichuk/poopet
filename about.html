We’ll Take Care of your Pets Friends
How do I give my cat a bath if she has long clumps of fur?

I suggest combing through your cat's fur with a cat brush to gently remove shredded hair that is still attached, just be gentle and do not pull at the fur. If that doesn't work, you can bathe her or take her to a professional groomer.

My dog is not good to people, but he is cute and friendly to us! What can I do?

Try warming your dog up to a friend. Show the friend affection, like hugging or cuddling. Then the dog will realize that other people are to be treated nicely.

How can I make my dog look better if it is a street dog?

For starters, give your dog a bath if you haven't already. Make sure you use warm water. Small touches like a nice, colorful collar with a bow or some other decoration can make a difference in a dog's appearance.

How do I take care of my pet?

Give your pet proper food, water and shelter, take him to the vet when needed and give him plenty of love and exercise.
